kitfo

Description

Kitfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish made from raw minced beef, seasoned with a variety of spices and clarified butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of raw red meat (traditionally lean beef)
  • 250 grams of niter kibbeh
  • 1 tablespoon of mitmita
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon of salt or to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the meat
  2. Prepare the Nitter kibbeh
  3. Season the meat
  4. Mix thoroughly
  5. Serve
